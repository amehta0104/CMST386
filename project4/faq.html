FAQ

Q : Do we cater?

A: Yes we cater for parties no larger than 50 people

Q : How can I place an order?

A: Orders can be placed on this website. Go to the store page and add whatever you would like to your cart, and checkout.

Q : How long will it take to fulfill our order?

A: You will receive a call from us when the order is placed to get an exact number. Small orders can be done in just a couple days, but larger orders can take up to 2 weeks depending on the size (catering included)
