In the new and ever evolving mobile world, responsive design is becoming increasingly more important. Instead of designing and hosting two seperate websites for your consumers, using responsive design allowss the company to save time and money, while also allowing their consumers to have the best user experience, whether they are consuming content on a regular desktop or on their mobile devices.
