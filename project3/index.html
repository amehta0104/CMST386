Explain the importance of responsive design and its impact on the target audience.

In the new and ever evolving mobile world, responsive design is becoming increasingly more important. With mobile phones and tablets dominating the internet these days, responsive design is becoming alsmot nessacery to run a successful business. Instead of designing and hosting two seperate websites for your consumers, using responsive design allowss the company to save time and money, while also allowing their consumers to have the best user experience, whether they are consuming content on a regular desktop or on their mobile devices.
